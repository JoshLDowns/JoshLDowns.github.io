Emo Robot

Emo Robot is a character that has worn many hats over the years. I started drawing him way back in highschool, and he evolved from there over the years. In college I ran a web comic on Tumblr (dating myself here) about him, and had a couple small art shows with a lot of work focusing on him. In recent years I started a small project called Emo Robot Gaming. I started incorporating my robot friend into Magic: The Gathering accessories and Twitch art assets.
