Emo Robot

Emo Robot is a character that has worn many hats over the years. I started drawing him way back in highschool, and he evolved from there. In college I ran a web comic on Tumblr (dating myself here) about him, and had a couple small art shows with a lot of work focusing on him. In recent years I started a small project called Emo Robot Gaming where I started incorporating my robot friend into Magic: The Gathering accessories and Twitch art assets.

Marketing

Over the years as a restaurant manager I spent a lot of time focusing on event planning (specifically gaming events) and promotion. I have been in charge of social media for a few establishments, and have made a lot of marketing materials like posters, flyers, web banners, and web advertisements.

Twitch Assets

A big hobby of mine over the past couple years has been streaming Magic: The Gathering on Twitch. I designed and created all of my overlays, emotes, and video assets myself for my channel, Emo Robot Gaming. I did a few commissions for other channels as well.

Genco Lab

Genco Lab is a part of the Boston University Medical Campus, and I do diagrams for some of the journals they publish.
